My Favorite Playlist

Cinnamon Sugar

Johnson

“Cinnamon Sugar” depicts the sweetness of discovering affection, relationship, and passion. It is a heartwarming story about a newfound love.

Daylight

Taylor Swift

“Daylight” is about leaving a time of darkness and embracing forgiveness for yourself and others.

Best Part

Daniel Caesar
(feat. H.E.R.)

“Best Part” is a soulful ode to love, celebrating how a special person makes life complete.

Sining

Dionela
(feat. Jay R)

“Sining” is the song he wrote as a final message to his loved ones, reflecting on mortality and his deep feelings for her girlfriend, who inspired it while playing her piano.

Die For You

The Weeknd
(feat. Ariana Grande)

“Die For You” is a powerful and emotional song that explores the theme of unconditional love and sacrifice.

double take

Dhruv

“double take” is a song about falling in love with a friend and how dizzying that can feel.

Die With A Smile

Lady Gaga
(feat. Bruno Mars)

“Die With A Smile” is a poignant exploration of love's enduring power in the face of life's fragility.

Snooze

SZA

“Snooze” explores the intense emotions of love, loyalty, and the fear of missing important moments with a partner.

Come Inside Of My Heart

IV OF SPADES

"Come Inside Of My Heart” explores themes of love, vulnerability, and the desire for deep connection.

Happy w u

Arthur Nery
(feat. Jason Dhakal)

“Happy w u” is all about a love so simple yet so affectionate that it brings pure bliss. It's a song that spells out how love can also be effortless.

Palagi

"Palagi” talks about someone promising to always be there for their loved one, no matter what may happen.

Tahanan

Adie

“Tahanan” is a song about lovers who finally found their comfort places for each other.

Photograph

Ed Sheeran

“Photograph” represents love, longing, memories, healing and hope.

Happiness

Rex Orange County

“Happiness” shows that feeling or caring for someone even when you are unsure about yourself. Wanting to give the best you can to someone you love.

Get You

Daniel Caeser
(feat. Kali Uchis)

“Get You” is a song about feeling grateful for a love that feels too good to be true. It’s about being with someone you really care about and wondering how you got so lucky to be with them.

Give Me Your Forever

Zack Tabudlo

“Give Me Your Forever” is a cute and romantic representation of how someone genuinely loves a person, and how love can make you do anything for the sake of your loved ones’ happiness.

seasons

wave to earth

“seasons” explores the conflicting feelings of love and grief. according to the song’s lyrics, the narrator is in love with someone but is afraid to be with them for fear that they would ruin their life.

Always

Daniel Caeser

“Always” is a post-breakup jam that hits you right in the feels. Even though his lover has moved on to another guy, he can't seem to let her go.

Save Your Tears

The Weeknd

"Save Your Tears” explores themes of heartbreak, emotional pain, and the facade people sometimes maintain in relationships. The song reflects on the struggle of moving on after a breakup while still feeling the weight of past emotions.

Sanctuary

Joji

"Sanctuary” describes where he is in love with a girl but he's telling the girl to not wait for him. But he is aiming for something greater but it's not really what he needs.

Reminder

The Weeknd

airplane thoughts

"airplane thoughts” is about saying goodbye to a lover, with no certainty of when you’ll see them next.

From The Start

Laufey

"From The Start” describes the awkwardness that takes hold when we’re in the presence of someone we secretly like.

You'll Be Safe Here

Rico Blanco

"You'll Be Safe Here” emphasize the importance of having someone to lean on during tough times, highlighting the need for love, warmth, and strength.
